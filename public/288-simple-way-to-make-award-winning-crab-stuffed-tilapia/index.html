The ingredients needed to make Crab Stuffed Tilapia:
  1. Take 5 tilapia fillets
  2. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup yellow onion (chopped)
  4. Get 2 cloves minced garlic
  5. Make ready 1 cup crabmeat (immitation or fresh)
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp cream cheese
  7. Prepare 1 tsp old bay seasoning
  8. Get to taste Salt & pepper
  9. Take 2 tbsp finely chopped garlic chives
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  11. Get Lemon juice
Instructions to make Crab Stuffed Tilapia:
  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞
  2. In medium sauce pan add olive oil & heat to medium heat. Add in onions until the start to caramelize a little bit. Then add garlic, chopped up crabmeat, cream cheese, old bay seasoning, s&p and garlic chives. Stir til well incorporated. Remove crab mixture from heat & allow to cool.
  3. Lay out tilapia fillets, pat dry with paper towel. Layer a thick layer of crabmeat mix onto each fillet, spread until even.
  4. Roll up each fillet & lay seam side down on a greased baking pan.
  5. Melt butter in a small cup. Mix melted butter with lemon juice, a tad of old bay seasoning, s&p & drizzle over the tops of rolled up crab stuffed fish.
  6. Bake for 20-25 mins or until fish starts to easily flake with a fork. Every 10 mins drizzle a little lemon/butter mix over fish during cooking process. Serve & Enjoy!!üç£
