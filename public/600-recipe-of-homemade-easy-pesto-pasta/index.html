The ingredients needed to make Easy Pesto Pasta:
  1. Make ready 1 bag Spinach
  2. Make ready 2 pkgs of Italian sausage links 1hot/1mild
  3. Get 3 bell peppers red, yellow, orange
  4. Prepare 2 boxes pasta (I prefer bowtie)
  5. Get 1 small jar of pesto
  6. Take Crushed red pepper for taste
  7. Prepare 1 pot
  8. Make ready 1 deep skillet
Instructions to make Easy Pesto Pasta:
  1. Boil water, and dice the peppers
  2. Pan fry sausages until browned
  3. Coin the sausages and then toss back in frying pan
  4. Toss in peppers and bag of spinach until spinach is reduced.
  5. Toss pasta in when water boils. Cook to desired firmness. (I prefer al dente)
  6. Mix pasta and sausage mix, along with jar of pesto. Season with crushed red pepper for a kick. Serve with garlic or regular bread.
