The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon and Shrimp:
  1. Make ready Salmon
  2. Take Shrimp peeled and deveined (raw or cooked)
  3. Take McCormick's Lemon Butter Dill Sauce
  4. Prepare Morton's All Purpose Seasoning
  5. Get Old Bay Seafood Seasoning
Instructions to make Baked Salmon and Shrimp:
  1. Turn broiler on and place oven rack on second level from top
  2. Place salmon skin side down on aluminum foil lined pan. Sprinkle liberally with All Purpose Seasoning and Old Bay Seasoning. Lightly coat with lemon butter sauce.
  3. Place in oven and broil for 12 minutes. Remove and add shrimp around salmon and season the same as the salmon. Place back in oven for 5-7 minutes or until desired doneness.
