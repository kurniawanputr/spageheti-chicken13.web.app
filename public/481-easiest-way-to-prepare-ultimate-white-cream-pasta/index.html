The ingredients needed to make White Cream Pasta:
  1. Get 1 box spaghetti
  2. Get 150 ml water
  3. Take 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  4. Make ready half a teaspoon of black pepper
  5. Prepare Half a cup of thickened cream
  6. Make ready 1 quarter cup of grated parmesan cheese
  7. Get 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon olive oil
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon minced garlic
Instructions to make White Cream Pasta:
  1. In a medium pot boil the water and add half the salt
  2. After the water boil add the pasta and wait around 10 minutes
  3. When the pasta is ready strain it and toss it in olive oil
  4. In a medium saucer add the cream, parmesan,olive oil, garlic,salt pepper and butter, and stir until its thickened.
  5. Plate and sereve.
  6. ENJOY!!!
