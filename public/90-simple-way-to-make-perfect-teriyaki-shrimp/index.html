The ingredients needed to make Teriyaki Shrimp:
  1. Get 1 lb shrimp
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp soy
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  4. Get 1 egg slightly beaten
  5. Prepare to taste salt, pepper, garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp cornstarch
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp sesame oil, or olive oil

Steps to make Teriyaki Shrimp:
  1. Rinse shrimp and pat dry. In a medium bowl combine egg, soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, & cornstarch.
  2. Add shrimp, stirring to coat, marinate 10 minutes.
  3. In a wok or large skillet, turn heat to medium high and add oil. Cook shrimp until golden brown, not to over cook.
  4. Arrange shrimp on shredded lettuce or fried rice. Enjoy!
