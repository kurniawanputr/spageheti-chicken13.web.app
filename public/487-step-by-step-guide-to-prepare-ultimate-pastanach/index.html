The ingredients needed to make Pastanach:
  1. Get 1 500 grams any kind of pasta
  2. Prepare 3 medium tomato (red over ripe) chopped
  3. Make ready 1 pack frozen chopped spinach
  4. Prepare 250 grams of mince beef (i used australian beef)
  5. Make ready 1 bulb onion chopped
  6. Get 3 gloves garlic-chopped
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon crushed pepper
  8. Make ready 1 beef cube in a 1 cup of water
  9. Take 1 teaspoon of basil

Steps to make Pastanach:
  1. Boil the pasta until it will cooked(don't over cooked) .at the same time cook the mince beef in a deep pan with 2 tablespoons of olive oil. ADD pepper and little salt enough to taste the meat. Cook the meat till brown and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add 2 tablespoon of olive oil, saute the garlic and onion until onion turns to brown then add the chopped tomatoes and the frozen spinach and let it boil in a medium fire until the ice melted and the tomatoes are well done.
  3. If you feel the mixture is already mixed well add the meat,beef broth, basil and salt to adjust the taste.
  4. Put the sauce over the pasta and mix it. Serve hot .
