Instructions to make Mom's love pasta:
  1. Boiled the pasta for 3 minutes
  2. After it half boil add cut broccoli in to the boiling pasta
  3. Drain the pasta with broccoli mixer
  4. Place a pan add butter
  5. Fry the egg and make it crushed
  6. Add pasta with broccoli mixer in to that
  7. Add pinch of turmeric and salt for the taste
  8. Last add slice cheese by braking small pcs
