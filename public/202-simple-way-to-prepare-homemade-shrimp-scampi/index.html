The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Get 12 Oz medium uncooked peeled shrimp
  2. Get 1 bell pepper
  3. Get 1 onion
  4. Take 3 Roma tomatoes seeded and chopped
  5. Take 1/3 cup butter
  6. Make ready 1-1/2 tbs minced garlic
  7. Make ready 2 tbs lemon juice
  8. Get 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  9. Prepare 1 tsp crushed red pepper
  10. Take 2 tbs olive oil

Steps to make Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Heat oil and butter until melted
  2. Saute onion, bell pepper, and garlic until onions are translucent
  3. Prepare noodles AL dente
  4. Add shrimp to saute onion/pepper/garlic
  5. Add vinegar, lemon, and crushed red pepper
  6. Cook until shrimp is done
  7. Add noodles and tomatoes to the pan and toss on low heat to mix it all up.
  8. If it's not saucy enough, add more olive oil/butter.
