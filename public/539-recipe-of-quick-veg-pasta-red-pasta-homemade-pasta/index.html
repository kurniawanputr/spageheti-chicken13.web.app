The ingredients needed to make Veg pasta-red pasta-homemade pasta:
  1. Prepare 1 cup pasta
  2. Take 1 tbsp oil
  3. Prepare As per taste salt
  4. Take 1 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  6. Take 1 tsp pizza seasoning
  7. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 chopped capsicum
  9. Get 1/2 chopped carrot
  10. Prepare 1-2 tbsp corn kernels
  11. Get 1/2 chopped onion
  12. Get Few cloves garlic
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp pizza pasta sauce
  14. Take 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  15. Get 1 tbsp grated cheese

Steps to make Veg pasta-red pasta-homemade pasta:
  1. First boil water in a pan add little salt, 1/4tbsp oil.
  2. Then add pasta,stir and cover with the lid. Check in between. Once it is done. Strain the extra water
  3. Wash the boiled pasta with cold water two to three times and keep aside.
  4. In a pan add remaining oil and butter.
  5. Add garlic, onion & sauté it.
  6. Then add remaining veggies, spices and sauces.
  7. Add pasta and mix well.
  8. Add grated cheese and serve.
