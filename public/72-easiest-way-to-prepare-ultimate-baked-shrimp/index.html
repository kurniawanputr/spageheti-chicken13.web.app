Steps to make Baked shrimp:
  1. Melt butter in pan.
  2. Slice lemon and place it on top of butter.
  3. Add fresh shrimp.
  4. Sprinkle seasoning over all.
  5. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
